General Notice

Welcome To Online Crime Report and Analysis System. This Service is made available by the Government of Nepal. It is designed to Make people of Nepal aware from crime. Here People can make online Complaints to the Police Station. In order to do so first they have to Register themselves on the system. User can also see the list of Most Wanted Criminals in various area through this web application. Here you can also see the detail of Missing person so you can contact to the person if you find such persons..
